ABOUT

Hi!
For my activist toolkit project, I have chosen the topic Student Suicide in India. This is a major problem plaguing our country and it spreads without much media coverage. I have made this website as a part of the Girls Who Code Virtual Summer Immersion Program 2021 in hopes of spreading awareness and destigmatising the topic of suicide in India.

Cheerio!
Kuhu Goel

Some of the main features of my website are-

  1. The pages are responsive and shrink/grow to fit the size of your screen. (Make it responsive)
  2. I have created a fact button to show facts on the home page. (Fact generator)
  3. The "Find Help" page has text input boxes where you can type your name and it shows personalised positive affirmations. (Personalise it)
  4. When the website is loaded, a trigger warning is shown. Also the background is blurred out.
  5. The image on the home page can be clicked to increase its size.
  6. When buttons are clicked, the page automatically scrolls to show content.
  7. Alt text has been added to all images and links to provide accessibility.
